Welcome to Cross CTF 2017

Cross CTF 2017 is a cyber security competition by SMU Whitehat Society & NUS Greyhats that will be held in Singapore Management University.

In the competition, student teams from various tertiary institutions will compete to solve challenges. Teams will compete in a 48 hour online qualifiers on the 13th-14th May and the qualifiying teams will compete in Singapore's first 24 hour live attack-defense CTF in the final stage on 17th-18th June.

Competition Details

This competition is open to all students from various institutes of higher learning and polytechnics in Singapore. Participants are required to form a team of three to take part in the competition. Each team must achieve a minimum qualifying score in the qualifier round to be allowed to participate in the competition.

Participants are required to bring and utilize their own laptop(s) and equipment(s) for any activity/competition.


Qualifiers Details

The qualifiers for Cross CTF will begin from 13 May 2017 until 14 May 2017, lasting 48 hours in total.

More details will be released through e-mail at a later date.


Rules of Cross CTF

During the CTF

  • Do not attack the PCs of other competitors.
  • Do not attack the scoring server.
  • Do not attack anything outside of the CTF environment.
  • Do not delete or modify flags.
  • ARP poisoning or any form of man-in-the-middle attack is prohibited. This action is subject to disqualification.
  • Sharing flags or solutions is prohibited. This action is subject to disqualification.
  • Brute-forcing flags will not be tolerated.
  • Denial of service attacks on any challenge will not be tolerated, unless the challenge calls for such an attack.
  • If you find any bug in the system, let us know. We might give you some bonus points.
  • Organizers' decision is final.
